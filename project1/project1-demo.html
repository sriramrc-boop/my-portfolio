Vehicle Blind Spot Indicator

The video above demonstrates the working of the Vehicle Blind Spot Indicator project. The system uses two ultrasonic sensors mounted on either side of the vehicle to continuously monitor the blind spot regions. These sensors detect nearby objects or vehicles within a predefined distance and transmit this data to an Arduino microcontroller.

Based on the sensor input, the Arduino processes the information and activates LED indicators corresponding to the side where a vehicle is detected. These visual alerts provide the driver with real-time feedback, helping them identify vehicles approaching from blind spots and improving overall driving safety.
